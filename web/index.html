Q&A

tempdecal.wad is a spray format for GoldSrc engine games such as Sven Co-op, Counter-Strike 1.6, and Half-Life. Only preset sprays are available by default. By replacing tempdecal.wad, you can use any images for sprays, even full color images!

That's the why this page exists. Drag and drop your favorite image, you will get your custom tempdecal.wad in a sec.

Here's the summary of folders. Copy tempdecal.wad to the specified folder.

Sven Co-op
[Game Folder]\svencoop
Counter-Strike 1.6
[Game Folder]\cstrike
Half-Life
[Game Folder]\valve

To open [Game Folder], go to Steam Library, right-click your game, then select Manage > Browse local files.

Besides, you should set tempdecal.wad Read-only to avoid it to be overwritten by the game. To set it Read-only, right-click the file, open Properties, check Read-only, then click OK.

That's it! You can use your custom spray right now.
